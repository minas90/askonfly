Different kind of Entrepreneurship

I’ve worked as a software developer for the last 10+ years. I started my career in a outsourcing company, after a while I quit and worked as a freelancer for a while. Then I worked in few startups both as Android and iOS developer.

About a year ago Facebook recruiter contacted me with a job offer. But you know what? After I passed the initial interviews I didn’t contact them back, also I quit my job to fully work on my personal project. Currently I’m a cofounder in a startup. We live in a country where getting a reasonable funding is close to impossible. So in a year I spent almost all my savings in the hope of success to get some traction, so we can get some funding from investors outside our country.

After almost 10 months of hard work we figured out we can’t grow without spending a lot of money in marketing. So we pivoted and pivoted and pivoted…

Finally we decided to start from bottom up. We gonna outsource for a while until we have enough resources and maybe big enough team to work on our personal project again.

Toptal looks really promising for finding interesting challenging projects on which only skilled people can work.

I think it’s going to be a great new beginning for us. We can help startups with building products, figuring out features for MVP, measuring product metrics and suggest tweaks for better Product/market fit.
